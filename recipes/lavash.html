Lavash

Lavash is the variation of Tukey's famous dish - kebab.

Lavash has almost similar structure to Turkish kebab, but its own unique sauce that is available only in Uzbekistan.

There are many ways to cook lavash, but my favorite one is to use as much sauce as possible.

First, prepare your spit and mean. When meat is cooked, put in on a nice slice of lavash. Don't forget to add some vegetables and sauce. Finally, wrap it up and eat!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Lavash
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook your meat on spit.
  2. Take it off and put on lavash.
  3. Add vegetables.
  4. Add sauce.
  5. Wrap it.
  6. Eat!
